About Us

Powering Odisha's Digital Backbone

POS Technologies was established in 2014 and has grown as the premier Barcode and RFID solution provider in Odisha. Our tradition of quality business in the Barcode solution market has made us the go-to single vendor for end-to-end solutions across both Barcode and RFID technologies.

We serve the Government Sector, Retail Automation, Manufacturing, Industrials, Asset Tracking, Library Automation, Attendance Monitoring, Transport Logistics, Inventory Management, Supply Chain Management, Hospitality Management and Healthcare.
